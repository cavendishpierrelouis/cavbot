Frequently asked questions

This is a living FAQ surface. Start with these, then grow it with real questions from your pilots and production teams.

  • How does CavBot decide what to watch first?
    CavBot starts with the routes and flows you mark as high-value: checkouts, sign-ups, and journeys where a broken link or slow response has real cost. You pick the routes; CavBot handles the 404s, structure, and runtime feel.
  • Is there a difference between support and pilots?
    Yes. support@cavbot.io handles everyday questions and account issues. pilots@cavbot.io is for structured, engineering-led pilots where we’re instrumenting or reshaping a flow together with your team.
  • Who do I contact about pricing or limits?
    For plan questions, limits, and invoices, use billing@cavbot.io. If you’re scoping a large pilot or need a custom footprint, you can copy pilots@cavbot.io for joint review.
  • How do I report a security issue?
    Please send a short, clear report to security@cavbot.io with reproduction steps and any relevant logs. We’ll acknowledge quickly and coordinate a secure path forward.
